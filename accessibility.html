Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 15, 2026

At Flekona, we are fully committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all visitors, including individuals with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive disabilities. We continuously optimize our e-commerce environment to provide a barrier-free user experience.

1. Conformance Status

To assist in making our digital store an equitable space, we target adherence to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines define best-practice frameworks to make digital content more accessible for screen readers, keyboard-only navigation, and other assistive technologies.

2. Core Accessibility Architecture

Our platform incorporates engineering principles designed to make buying simple and accessible, including:

  • Semantic HTML Structure: Logical document grouping to ensure assistive screen-reading equipment interprets layout sequencing accurately.
  • Consistent Contrast Ratios: Text elements styled purposefully against the pure white background to maintain readable visual hierarchies.
  • Alternative Visual Text: Integration of concise descriptive labels on key images to communicate non-text elements cleanly.

3. Ongoing Optimizations

Digital accessibility requires persistent assessment. We routinely review the codebase and interface components to remedy compatibility problems introduced during browser ecosystem shifts or design updates.

4. Feedback & Assistance

We welcome your insights regarding the accessibility of our platform. If you encounter structural, accessibility, or technical barriers while exploring our products, please connect with our support services. We will coordinate promptly to provide the required solutions or items through an alternative channel.
