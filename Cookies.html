Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Flekona uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to recognize you when you visit our platform. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by online platform operators to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for technical reasons in order for our platform to operate, and we refer to these as "essential" or "strictly necessary" cookies. Other cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our marketplace.

3. Types of Cookies We Deploy

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential to provide you with services available through our platform and to use some of its features, such as maintaining items inside your shopping cart or securing user accounts.
  • Performance & Functionality Cookies: These cookies are used to enhance the performance and functionality of our platform but are non-essential to their use (for example, remembering your language or currency preference).
  • Analytics & Customization Cookies: These collect data that helps us understand how our platform is being used or how effective our marketing campaigns are, so we can continuously tailor the setup for you.

4. How Can You Control Cookies?

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though your access to some functionality and areas of our platform may be restricted (like staying logged into an account or saving cart configurations).

5. Policy Revisions

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please re-visit this policy regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies and related technologies.
