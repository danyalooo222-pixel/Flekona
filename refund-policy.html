Flekona Refund and Returns Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Standard Evaluation Window

Buyers hold a structured window of 30 days immediately following product arrival metrics to declare a formal claim inquiry. Returned item conditions must arrive back at matching localized supply warehouses unmodified to approve full balance processing checks.

2. Digital Capital Allocations

For items cataloged under our direct digital creator programs (including independent self-published materials and assets), claims are evaluated individually within 48 business hours to verify that file downloading paths or tracking authentications failed to execute correctly.

3. Cross-Border Payout Streams

Approved return validations generate immediate payment signals inside our billing gateway layers. Depending on regional payment configurations or specific banking provider parameters, cleared funds generally restabilize back to your original source configuration within 5 to 7 operational days.
