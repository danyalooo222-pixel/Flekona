Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

At Flekona, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your information when you visit or make a purchase from our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our platform, we collect certain details necessary to process your transactions and improve your experience, including:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, shipping address, billing address, email address, and phone number.
  • Device Details: IP address, browser type, time zone, and data regarding how you browse our product offerings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect internally to fulfill the core operations of our platform, specifically to:

  • Fulfill, ship, and track orders placed through the site.
  • Screen our platform transactions for potential risk or fraudulent activity.
  • Communicate with you regarding customer support requests or transactional updates.

3. Data Protection & Sharing

Your privacy is strictly protected. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share data with essential third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, such as shipping carriers (to deliver your products) and encrypted payment gateways (to process transactions securely).

4. Cookies & Trackers

We use essential cookies to maintain your active shopping sessions, remember items added to your cart, and understand how visitors interact with our layout. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though doing so may impact certain functionalities of our storefront.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, request that incorrect details be corrected, or ask for your personal data to be permanently deleted from our active databases.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy statement occasionally to reflect changes to our operational, legal, or regulatory guidelines. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.
